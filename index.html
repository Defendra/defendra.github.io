  • Why should we trust you with my security?

    The internal testing team at Defendra consists of seasoned physical security penetration testers implementing years of research and experience into each consultation in the aim of providing the best result.

  • What is a penetration tester?

    A penetration tester assesses computer systems' security by simulating cyber attacks to identify vulnerabilities and help organizations strengthen their defenses; this often overlaps into real-world security through the form of physical pen-tests.
