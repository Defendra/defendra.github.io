What Are Door/Window Sensors?

Door/window sensors are discreet devices designed to detect the opening and closing of doors and windows within your home. They consist of two main components: a sensor installed on the frame of the door or window and a magnet placed on the corresponding moving part. When the door or window is opened, the magnetic connection is broken, triggering the sensor to send a signal to your security system's control panel.
