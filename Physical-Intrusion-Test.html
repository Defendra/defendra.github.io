Overview of Our Physical Intrusion Testing Services

In today's world, where security breaches are increasingly common, ensuring your physical security is robust and effective is essential. Our Physical Intrusion Testing service helps you identify and mitigate vulnerabilities in your physical security infrastructure. Through simulated real-world break-ins and comprehensive assessments, we offer actionable insights to fortify your defenses and safeguard your premises.

Get in Touch
Simulated Break-Ins: Uncover Weaknesses in Your Security

We start with meticulously planned simulated break-ins. Our security experts attempt to breach your premises using tactics employed by actual intruders. This hands-on testing reveals weaknesses in locks, surveillance systems, entry control systems, and other physical barriers. In a recent test, our experts were able to breach a facility's security within minutes, highlighting critical vulnerabilities in their surveillance system and entry controls. By simulating such scenarios, we can pinpoint exactly where your security measures might fail and offer precise recommendations for improvement.

Confidentiality Tests: Protecting Sensitive Information

Security isn't just about keeping unauthorized people out—it's also about protecting sensitive information. During our confidentiality tests, we try to access sensitive documents or connect unauthorized devices to your network. This process highlights potential security gaps that could be exploited by malicious actors. For example, during a confidentiality test, we successfully accessed sensitive documents in an unsecured office area, underscoring the need for better document security protocols. Whether it's an unlocked file cabinet or an unsecured Wi-Fi network, our tests will uncover these vulnerabilities.

Staff Training Assessment: Enhancing Employee Vigilance

Your staff is your first line of defense against intrusions. We evaluate how well-trained your employees are in detecting and responding to unauthorized access attempts. This assessment involves testing their reactions to suspicious activities, their adherence to security protocols, and their overall awareness of potential threats. Our assessments revealed that well-trained staff can prevent up to 80% of unauthorized access attempts. The insights gained help you understand how to improve your training programs and enhance employee vigilance.

Detailed Reporting: Actionable Insights for Improved Security

After completing our tests, we provide a thorough report detailing all identified vulnerabilities. This report includes actionable recommendations to enhance your security posture. Our goal is to not only identify weaknesses but also to empower you with the knowledge and strategies needed to address them effectively. Our detailed reports cover all identified vulnerabilities, along with practical recommendations. For instance, a recent report included specific advice on upgrading lock systems, enhancing surveillance coverage, and improving staff response protocols.
