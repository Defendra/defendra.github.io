Service

What do we offer?

Our Services Include:

1. Installation and Setup: Our team of skilled technicians is proficient in installing Door Entry Systems from the ground up. We meticulously plan the installation process, ensuring seamless integration with your property's infrastructure. Whether it's a new construction project or a retrofitting endeavor, we have the expertise to deliver reliable and efficient installations.

2. Reuse of Existing Wiring: If your property already has a Door Entry System in place, we can leverage the existing wiring infrastructure to minimize disruption and cost. Our technicians conduct a thorough assessment of the existing setup to determine compatibility and feasibility. By repurposing the existing wiring, we streamline the installation process while maintaining the integrity of your property's infrastructure.

3. Customization and Configuration: We understand that every property has unique access control requirements. That's why we offer customizable solutions tailored to suit your specific needs. From audio-only intercom systems to advanced video entry systems with remote access capabilities, we work closely with you to design a solution that aligns with your security goals and budget.

4. Maintenance and Support: Our commitment to excellence extends beyond installation. We offer ongoing maintenance and support services to ensure the continued reliability and functionality of your Door Entry System. Whether it's troubleshooting technical issues or performing routine inspections, our team is dedicated to keeping your access control system operating at peak performance.
